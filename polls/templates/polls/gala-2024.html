Each year, we gather at our Annual Awards Gala for an evening filled with gratitude and inspiration. We reflect on our successes of the past year and honor the CCEE advocates who enable us to keep aspiring towards a better future. We take this time to showcase the partners who invested all of the above and more this year. Without them we could not do what we do.

And so, we cordially invite you to join us again as we recognize and celebrate our accomplishments and the volunteers, educators, and supporters who made them possible. On Thursday, April 18th, 2024 at the Skirball Cultural Center in Los Angeles, our Annual Gala will toast to 65 Years of Economic Education. We hope to see you there!
